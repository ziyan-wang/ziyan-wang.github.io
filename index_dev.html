Short Biography

Ziyan is a full stack developer and researcher.

Ziyan devotes himself to make programming languages and tools better. Specifically, he wants to simplify the way people learn programming and the way developers work, by designing sugared general-purpose and domain-specific languages with high development productivity and diverse programming paradigms.

Despite being a researcher, Ziyan values practice. He considers himself both a thinker and a doer: he usually implements his ideas on his own, rather than asking his peers for help.
